    Is Online Poker Legal in the United States?

    Despite what you may have heard, playing online poker in the United States is NOT illegal. I repeat, online poker is not federally illegal in the USA. The only states that have laws making online poker illegal are in Washington and Louisiana (though no individuals have ever been indicted or prosecuted in any US jurisdiction).

    In 2013, a few states officially regulated online poker in the USA. Nevada, New Jersey, and Delaware were the first states to legalize the game online. More recently, Pennsylvania and Michigan regulated online poker as well. It's important to note that if you play on these sites you will only be playing against players in those states. If you wish to play online poker with players from other parts of the world you will need to play on sites like Bovada, Intertops, BetOnline, and Blackchip Poker. All of these sites have a long history of being reliable and fair and in many ways are better than the regulated state by state poker sites.
